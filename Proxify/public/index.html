WHy proXIFY ?

"Solution to mandatory 75% attendance in our college"

We all have been stuck in many situations where even after trying our level best and spending our useful time, we ended up without being able to manage for our proxy, or suddenly one day two people raise their hands for a single person. 75 percent attendance in colleges is a headache ....  you aren't even able to take your girlfriend out for a whole day ; ) .


                                                         If you are also facing the same problem then my friend we give you Proxify, a proxy managament web application that will assure your 75 percent attendance in any case. Now you won't have to call each and every person and say "Bhai proxy lagadio plsssss", you just have to mark it in the application and we'll assign a person who will mark your proxy in the class.


                                                          Wanna be the proxy king ? We feature a leaderboard that will show your position among your fellows and will be based on the number of tokens you currently have. But be cautious ....... if you end up exhausting all your tokens then there will be no possibility for you to get a proxy. So have fun : ) and let Proxify manage your proxies for the day .
