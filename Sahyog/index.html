Salient Features

One of the biggest problem in most developing countries is that the reach of doctors is limited and people from rural areas need to come to cities and towns nearby for proper treatment.

  • Here at Sahyog, we provide direct communication between you and the doctor which is completely confidential.
  • You are free to ask any problems related to your health to any professional of your choice.
  • The entire conversation will be over the Internet, so there is no need to travel to the doctor's clinic any more.
  • If you further want to meet the doctor, we can arrange an appointment for you.
