Why are drug stores imporatant?

Small commercial drug shops and pharmacies are often the first line of health care in poor countries, especially in rural areas that have very few private or public clinics. Drug shops and pharmacies, with their convenience, anonymity, and cost-savings (compared to private physicians), are an important source of health services, products, and information that is particularly important in the context of high maternal mortality and morbidity, critical health-worker shortages, poorly stocked clinics, and high unmet need for family planning

Where are generic medicine stores?

Generic medicine stores atr those drug stroes or pharmacies which are prescribed to sell generic medicines. A generic drug is a medication created to be the same as an already marketed brand-name drug in dosage form, safety, strength, route of administration, quality, performance characteristics, and intended use.

Due to low awareness of generic medicine in our society, these types of medicine stores are scarce.However with the effort of the government more and more such shops are being in different parts of country, specially in rural and impoverished areas in order to provide better healthcare services in these areas.
